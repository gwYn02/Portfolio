From the seas to screens:

An Officer's Transition to Analytics.

I was an officer onboard Ro-Ro ships for eight years navigating worldwide. Sailing for more than a decade was always a fun and fulfilling experience until Covid hit. My daughter was also born soon after. Shedding tears when I went back onboard, I knew I had to get out of my comfort zone.

Upon learning about data analytics, I knew it was something I would love to do. I immediately enrolled in a digital academy to gain the necessary skills. Nowadays, most of my free time are spent just improving what i learned. Doing something you enjoy, in the comfort of your home with the people you love is priceless.

I have been working on projects here to showcase my data analysis skills. I am excited to bring these new found competencies to the table and use them to help companies make data-driven decisions. I am confident that my passion for analytics and willingness to learn and adapt will make me a valuable addition to any team.

Yellevate Disputes Report

Tools Used: PostgreSQL, MS Excel, MS Office

Led a team of analyst students in identifying the problems facing the business and came up with business objectives that can solve them with data analysis. Yellevate has been struggling with client disputes. This has led to an approximate 5% annual loss of revenue (in USD). By analyzing data on disputes and identifying their causes, actionable strategies were developed to resolve them. The analysis focused on variables such as processing time for invoices and disputes, percentage of lost disputes and revenue, and the country with the highest losses from lost disputes.

Airbnb Dashboard

Tools Used: Power Query, Power BI (Personal Project)

Created a dashboard for Airbnb listings in NYC. The analysis focused to give information on factors that affect each listings. Data was enriched by adding calculated columns based on the assumption that location was a great factor. The dashboard could be beneficial for existing hosts wanting to expand and aspiring hosts deciding where to open a listing.
